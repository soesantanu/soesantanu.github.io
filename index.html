About

I am a joint PhD student in Mathematics at IIT Madras and the University of Melbourne. My research focuses on operator splitting algorithms, adaptive primal-dual methods, and their applications to inverse problems and machine learning. I am particularly interested in the Golden Ratio Primal-Dual Algorithm and its variants, along with structured regularization models such as GraphNet, elastic net generalizations, and robust imaging formulations.
